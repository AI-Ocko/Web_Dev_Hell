# TLDR
Better Call Saul stands as perhaps one of the greatest achievements of modern television history.
However, it is difficult to recommend this show to anyone who hasn't seen it's more popular predecessor, Breaking Bad.
Better Call Saul invariably relies on the pretext of Breaking Bad to get many of it's thematic points across.
Despite this, I do believe it's possible to watch this show alone, and appreciate it as a standalone series.
In fact, it could be said that shotgunning this entire franchise, with this series as the starter, could be an valid method of experiencing the entirety of the Breaking Bad universe.
Either way, Better Call Saul is a slow-burn deep-dive character study filled with themes of justice, morality, brotherhood, and descent into depravity.
